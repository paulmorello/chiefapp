Frequently Asked Questions

How does this product differ to a normal task management app?

Traditional task management apps are designed to help organize day to day tasks, they’re not designed to help you reach a your life goals. The Chief app will help you break down your important life goals into small tasks that you can start completing now.

What is the cost?

No cost, it’s free!

Does it matter what business I want to start?

No. You can have any business idea in mind. Chief will help you to think small and break it into small chunks.

What are some example business use cases?

You can use Chief for any type of business. You can use it to track the development of your app idea, keep on top of content for a blog or even the tasks required for a cafe.

Will I be able to receive reminders?

Yes. You can set reminders and receive notifications to keep you on track.

Is it hard to get started?

No. Set up is simple. Chief will ask you what type of business you want to start and then you can start creating your tasks.
